Terms of Service

By commissioning me, you agree to the following terms:

1. Payment & Process
Full Payment Upfront: Payment is required in full via available service before I begin the sketching process.
Workflow: I will provide a Sketch Preview and a Flat Color Preview for your approval during the process.
Revisions: You are entitled to 2 free revisions during the Sketch Phase. Any major anatomical or pose changes requested after the lineart or coloring has started will incur an additional fee ($3+ depending on complexity).

2. Usage Policy
Personal Use: You may use the artwork for social media profiles, wallpapers, personal prints, or as a gift. Please credit me as the artist.
Commercial Use: If you intend to use the art for profit (Merchandise, available service branding, etc.), a Commercial Fee will be applied. Please contact me for a custom quote before ordering.
Copyright: I retain the right to display the commissioned work in my portfolio or social media for self-promotion. If the character is a "Secret Reveal," please let me know in advance!

3. Delivery Details
Turnaround Time: Standard delivery is 3-7 days per piece. I will notify you immediately if there are any unexpected delays.
Files: You will receive a high-resolution PNG file. A (white/transparent) background version is upon request.

4. Cancellation & Refund Policy
Full Refund: Only available if I have NOT started the sketch.
Partial Refund (50%): Available if the sketch is finished but coloring has not yet started.
No Refund: Once the artwork is finished and delivered, no refunds will be issued.
Artist Cancellation: If I am unable to complete your commission for any reason, a 100% refund will be issued regardless of progress.
